Circuit Documentation
Summary
This circuit is designed to integrate multiple sensors and LEDs with a FireBeetle ESP32 microcontroller. It includes several HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Modules, a KY-018 LDR Photo Resistor, WS2812B LEDs, and a SN74AHCT125N buffer/driver. The circuit is powered by a 5V PSU and is intended for applications that require motion detection, light sensing, and LED control.
Component List
1. FireBeetle ESP32
   * Description: A microcontroller board based on the ESP32 chip, providing Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities.
   * Pins: RST/EN, LRCK/IO17, DI/IO16, DO/IO4, MCLK/IO12, BCLK/IO14, SCL/IO22, SDA/IO21, MISO/IO19, MOSI/IO23, SCK/IO18, IO0, NC, A4/IO15, A3/IO35, A2/IO34, A1/IO39/SENSOR_VN, A0/IO36/SENSOR_VP, D0/IO3/RXD, D1/IO1/TXD, D2/IO25, D3/IO26, D4/IO27, D5/IO9/SD2, D6/IO10/SD3, D7/IO13, D8/IO5, D9/IO2, IO6/CLK, IO7/SD0, IO8/SD1, IO11/SMD, GND, AREF, 3V3, VCC
2. HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module
   * Description: A mini passive infrared motion sensor module.
   * Pins: +, out, -
3. SN74AHCT125N
   * Description: A quadruple bus buffer gate with 3-state outputs.
   * Pins: 1OE, 1A, 1Y, 2OE, 2A, 2Y, GND, Vcc, 4OE, 4A, 4Y, 3OE, 3A, 3Y
4. WS2812B LED
   * Description: An individually addressable RGB LED.
   * Pins: +5V, DO, GND, Din
5. KY-018 LDR Photo Resistor
   * Description: A light-dependent resistor used for light sensing.
   * Pins: Signal, VCC, Ground
6. 5V PSU
   * Description: A power supply unit providing 5V output.
   * Pins: V+, V-, Ground, Neutral, Line
Wiring Details
FireBeetle ESP32
* VCC is connected to the V+ of the 5V PSU.
* GND is connected to the V- of the 5V PSU and the ground pins of other components.
* D7/IO13 is connected to 1A of the SN74AHCT125N.
* A2/IO34 is connected to the Signal pin of the KY-018 LDR Photo Resistor.
* LRCK/IO17 is connected to the out pin of one HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* D4/IO27 is connected to the out pin of another HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* MOSI/IO23 is connected to the out pin of another HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* MISO/IO19 is connected to the out pin of another HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* SCK/IO18 is connected to the out pin of another HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* DI/IO16 is connected to the out pin of another HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module.
* 3V3 is connected to the + pins of all HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Modules and the VCC of the KY-018 LDR Photo Resistor.
HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Module
* + pins are connected to the 3V3 of the FireBeetle ESP32.
* out pins are connected to various pins on the FireBeetle ESP32 as described above.
* - pins are connected to the V- of the 5V PSU.
SN74AHCT125N
* Vcc is connected to the V+ of the 5V PSU.
* GND is connected to the V- of the 5V PSU.
* 1A is connected to D7/IO13 of the FireBeetle ESP32.
* 1Y is connected to the Din of the first WS2812B LED.
WS2812B LED
* +5V is connected to the V+ of the 5V PSU.
* GND is connected to the V- of the 5V PSU.
* Din of the first LED is connected to 1Y of the SN74AHCT125N.
* DO of each LED is connected to the Din of the next LED in the chain.
KY-018 LDR Photo Resistor
* Signal is connected to A2/IO34 of the FireBeetle ESP32.
* VCC is connected to the 3V3 of the FireBeetle ESP32.
* Ground is connected to the V- of the 5V PSU.
5V PSU
* V+ is connected to the VCC of the FireBeetle ESP32, Vcc of the SN74AHCT125N, and +5V of the WS2812B LEDs.
* V- is connected to the GND of the FireBeetle ESP32, SN74AHCT125N, WS2812B LEDs, and the - pins of the HC-SR505 Mini PIR Motion Sensing Modules.
Code
There is no embedded code provided for this circuit. The functionality of the circuit relies on the hardware connections and the default behavior of the components. If code is required for specific operations, it should be developed and uploaded to the FireBeetle ESP32 as needed.
